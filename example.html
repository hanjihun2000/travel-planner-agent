Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Seoul (May 23)

  • ✈️ Arrive at Incheon International Airport (ICN) at 18:10
  • 🚝 Take the Airport Railroad (AREX) to Hongdae (approx. 1 hour).
  • 🏨 Check into your Airbnb near Hongdae station.
  • 🍽️ Dinner: Explore local restaurants in Hongdae for Korean BBQ or street food.

Day 2: Exploring Hongdae & Shopping (May 24)

  • 🛍️ Morning: Explore Hongdae's shopping streets, known for fashion boutiques, accessory shops, and cosmetics stores.
  • 🎨 Afternoon: Visit the Hongdae Free Market (usually open on weekends) to see local artists and craft vendors.
  • 🍜 Evening: Enjoy dinner at a local restaurant in Hongdae, known for its diverse culinary scene.

Day 3: Cultural Immersion (May 25)

  • 🏯 Morning: Visit Gyeongbokgung Palace, the largest of Seoul's Five Grand Palaces. Gyeongbokgung Palace
  • 🍵 Afternoon: Explore Bukchon Hanok Village, a traditional Korean village with beautiful historic houses. Bukchon Hanok Village
  • 🚶‍♀️ Evening: Stroll through Insadong, known for traditional crafts, tea houses, and art galleries.

Day 4: Myeongdong & Namsan Tower (May 26)

  • 🛍️ Morning: Shop in Myeongdong, a bustling shopping district with international brands and local boutiques.
  • 🍴 Lunch: Enjoy street food and local delicacies in Myeongdong.
  • 🗼 Afternoon: Visit Namsan Tower for panoramic views of Seoul. Namsan Tower

Day 5: DMZ Tour (May 27)

  • 🕊️ Full Day: Take a DMZ (Demilitarized Zone) tour to the border between North and South Korea. (Book in advance)
  • Dinner: Have dinner near your hotel.

Day 6: Departure (May 28)

  • ☕ Morning: Enjoy a final Korean breakfast at a local cafe.
  • ✈️ Depart from Incheon International Airport (ICN).

Local Tips

  • Learn basic Korean phrases.
  • Use the subway for efficient and affordable transportation.
  • Tipping is not customary in South Korea.
  • Try local street food like tteokbokki and kimchi.
  • Weather in late May is generally pleasant with temperatures ranging from 14°C to 23°C.
