Better to include the following information in your request to get the best results:

- Destination(s): [City, Country or multiple locations]
- Travel Dates: [Start date] to [End date]
- Number of Travelers: [Adults, children, any special group info]
- Traveler Profiles: [Brief info about travelers’ interests, experience level with destination, special needs]
- Budget: [Total or per person, currency]
- Accommodation Preferences: [Hotel, hostel, Airbnb, proximity to landmarks or transport, amenities, review quality]
- Transportation Preferences: [Preferred airlines, direct flights, train, car rental, etc.]
- Interests and Activities: [Cultural exploration, sightseeing, shopping, adventure, food, relaxation, etc.]
- Special Requests or Constraints: [Dietary restrictions, accessibility, group dynamics, etc.]
