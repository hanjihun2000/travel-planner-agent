Seoul Travel Itinerary 🌎

Overview

Dates: April 25, 2025 - April 29, 2025

Group Size: 2

Accommodation 🏨

Considering your budget, I recommend Hotel Skypark Central Myeongdong. It is a 4-star hotel with go reviews, close to Myeongdong subway station, and offers decent amenities.

  • Pros: Good location, free shuttle, good reviews, relatively affordable.
  • Cons: Can be crowded, rooms might be small.

    Daily Itinerary

    Day 1: Arrival & Myeongdong Exploration (April 25)

    • 13:35: Depart from HKG (Hong Kong International Airport) via Jeju Air flight 7C 6022.
    • 18:15: Arrive at ICN (Incheon International Airport).
    • 19:00: Take the Airport Railroad Express (AREX) to Seoul Station (approx. 50 mins, 9,500 KRW).
    • 20:00: Check in to Hotel Skypark Central Myeongdong.
    • 21:00: Dinner at Myeongdong Food Street: Try street food like tteokbokki, kimbap, and Korean fried chicken. 🍜
    • 22:00: Myeongdong shopping: Explore the various cosmetic shops and clothing stores. 🛍️

    Day 2: Historical Sites & Insadong (April 26)

    • 09:00: Breakfast at the hotel or a local cafe.
    • 10:00: Visit Gyeongbokgung Palace: Explore Seoul's largest and most beautiful royal palace (Admission: 3,000 KRW). 🏛️
    • 12:00: Lunch near Gyeongbokgung Palace: Try a traditional Korean meal.
    • 13:30: Explore Bukchon Hanok Village: Wander through the traditional Korean houses. 🏡
    • 15:00: Head to Insadong: Explore traditional crafts, tea houses, and art galleries. 🎨
    • 18:00: Dinner in Insadong: Try traditional Korean cuisine or a tea house experience.
    • 19:30: Check if there is a K-pop concert happening. Otherwise, enjoy a relaxing evening at a cafe. 🎤

    Day 3: Shopping in Gangnam & N Seoul Tower (April 27)

    • 09:00: Breakfast at the hotel or a local cafe.
    • 10:00: Head to Gangnam: Explore the upscale shopping district.
    • 11:00: Shopping at COEX Mall: Visit the Starfield Library and explore various shops. 📚
    • 13:00: Lunch in Gangnam: Try trendy cafes or restaurants.
    • 15:00: Visit N Seoul Tower: Take a cable car up to the tower for panoramic views of Seoul (Cab car: 14,000 KRW). 🗼
    • 18:00: Dinner near N Seoul Tower: Enjoy a meal with a view.
    • 20:00: Explore Itaewon: Enjoy the diverse nightlife and international cuisine. 🌙

    Day 4: Day Trip to DMZ (April 28)

    • 08:00: Join a DMZ (Demilitarized Zone) tour: Experience the unique and historical border betwe North and South Korea (approx. 40,000 - 50,000 KRW). 🇰🇷
    • 13:00: Lunch included in the DMZ tour.
    • 17:00: Return to Seoul.
    • 18:00: Dinner in Hongdae: Enjoy the youthful and artistic atmosphere.
    • 19:00: Explore Hongdae: Check out street performances and independent shops. 🕺

    Day 5: Departure (April 29)

    • 09:00: Breakfast at the hotel or a local cafe.
    • 10:00: Last-minute souvenir shopping.
    • 12:00: Check out from the hotel.
    • 13:00: Lunch near Seoul Station.
    • 14:00: Take the AREX to Incheon International Airport.
    • 18:15: Depart from ICN (Incheon International Airport) to HKG (Hong Kong International Airport).

Important Notes ℹ️

  • Purchase a T-money card for easy transportation on subways and buses.
  • Learn basic Korean phrases for better communication.
  • Check for K-pop concerts in advance and book tickets online.
  • Wear comfortable shoes as you'll be doing a lot of walking.
  • April is a pleasant time to visit Seoul, but pack layers as the temperature can fluctuate.

Booking Requirements 📋

  • Flights should be booked in advance to secure the best prices.
  • Hotel reservations should be made as soon as possible.
  • DMZ tour requires booking in advance.
  • Check for K-pop concert schedules and book tickets immediately if available.

Local Tips 🗺️

  • Try the local street food in Myeongdong and Hongdae.
  • Visit local markets like Namdaemun Market for unique souvenirs.
  • Explore the Cheonggyecheon Stream for a relaxing walk.
  • Be aware of Korean dining etiquette, such as not lifting your bowl from the table.
