Daily Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival & Myeongdong Exploration

Arrive at Incheon International Airport (ICN), take the AREX Express train to Seoul Station. Check into your accommodation. Head to Myeongdong for street food and shopping.

Day 2: Cultural Immersion - Palaces & Bukchon

Visit Gyeongbokgung Palace 🏯, explore Bukchon Hanok Village 🏘️, and visit the Insadong tea houses.

Day 3: Shopping Spree - Dongdaemun & Hongdae

Explore the Dongdaemun Design Plaza (DDP) and shop for the latest fashion trends. In the afternoon, head to Hongdae for unique boutiques and street performances.

Day 4: History & Views - DMZ & N Seoul Tower

Take a day trip to the Demilitarized Zone (DMZ) for a historical experience (book in advance!). In the evening, visit N Seoul Tower for panoramic city views.

Day 5: Food & Departure

Visit Gwangjang Market for a local food tour 🍲. Try Bindaetteok (mung bean pancake) and Mayak Gimbap (addictive seaweed rice rolls). Do some last-minute souvenir shopping before heading to the airport for your flight back to Hong Kong ✈️.
