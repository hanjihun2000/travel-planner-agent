Accommodations

Day 1: Arrival in Tokyo (May 23)

  • ✈️ Flight: Greater Bay Airlines HB 320, 09:20 - 15:05, HKG - NRT, HK$2006 total
  • 🏨 Check-in: lyf Shibuya Tokyo, 3:00 PM, Shibuya, Tokyo, Check-in: 3:00 PM, Check-out: 11:00 AM, Hotel Website
  • 🍴 Dinner: Explore Shibuya's Food Scene - Try Ramen or Izakaya, Estimated cost: HK$300

Day 2: Shibuya & Harajuku (May 24)

  • 🚶 Shibuya Crossing: Experience the iconic scramble crossing. Free
  • 🛍️ Shopping in Shibuya: Explore Shibuya 109 and other trendy shops. Budget: HK$1000
  • 🌲 Meiji Jingu Shrine (Harajuku): A peaceful oasis in the city. Free
  • Takeshita Street (Harajuku): Indulge in colorful street food and unique fashion. Budget: HK$500

Day 3: Culture in Asakusa & Sumida River Cruise (May 25)

  • ⛩️ Senso-ji Temple (Asakusa): Tokyo's oldest temple. Free
  • Nakamise-dori Street (Asakusa): Traditional crafts and snacks. Budget: HK$300
  • 🚢 Sumida River Cruise: Enjoy scenic views of Tokyo. Cost: HK$200 per person
  • Tokyo Skytree: Observe the city from above. Cost: HK$200 per person

Day 4: Ginza & Imperial Palace (May 26)

  • Ginza Shopping District: Explore high-end boutiques and department stores. Budget: HK$1000
  • Imperial Palace East Garden: A historic site with beautiful gardens. Free
  • Kabuki-za Theatre: Catch a traditional Kabuki performance (optional, advance booking recommended). Cost: HK$500-HK$1500 per person

Day 5: Day Trip to Hakone (May 27)

  • Hakone: A mountain resort town known for its natural beauty and views of Mount Fuji. Travel Cost: HK$500 per person
  • Lake Ashi Cruise: Scenic cruise on the lake. Cost: HK$200 per person
  • Hakone Open-Air Museum: Contemporary sculptures set against the backdrop of nature. Cost: HK$200 per person

Day 6: Departure (May 28)

  • 🏨 Check-out: lyf Shibuya Tokyo, 11:00 AM
  • ✈️ Flight: Greater Bay Airlines HB 320, NRT - HKG (Included in round-trip ticket)
